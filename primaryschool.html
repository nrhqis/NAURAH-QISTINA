PRIMARY SCHOOL

Being the introvert me, it is was a torture for me to go out and 'show' myself. But in the end, I'll still do it whether I like it or not But so far, the ones activity that I truly enjoyed was joining any activities related to Puteri Perlembagaan Islam Malaysia. Even though in the picture, I look like a twig about to crack into two anytime soon, I was actually quite hardy. I've joined camping contest, cooking contest, PPIM log books contest and many more that I've lost count. Every PPIM contest held, I would be in it, contesting. Although, at that time I felt so tired and quite burnt out, but I still gone through with it.

I might saya that primary school was me at my peak. Thanks to my mother for pushing me, of course. But one of my favourite contest of all time was marching contest. The day was sunny but right when it is my school turn to march, it rained heavily so suddenly. But we still pull through despite the weather. We kept on marching and we got first place for that contest.

All the sweat and tears that I've sacrificed were paid off when I got acknowledge as a figure in extracurricular.
